# 🔬 Research Experience and Projects

<div style="display: flex; justify-content: space-between; flex-wrap: wrap;">
<b>1. Generation Mechanism and Suppression Measures of Current Overshoot in Reversible Solid Oxide Cells (Key R&D Program of Shaanxi Province)</b>
<b style="white-space: nowrap;">2023.09 – 2024.12 Student Research Lead</b>
</div>
- A three-dimensional RSOC multi-physics field model was established based on COMSOL to reveal the overshoot mechanism during dynamic operation 
- A bidirectional adaptive fuzzy control strategy was designed in combination with Simulink to achieve smooth regulation of mode switching 
- Proposed gas supply optimization methods to suppress dynamic overshoot and enhance the operational stability of RSOC

<div style="display: flex; justify-content: space-between; flex-wrap: wrap;">
<b>2. Key Technologies of Co-Electrolysis for Long-Duration Energy Storage in Power Grids (Grid Company Science and Technology Project)</b>
<b style="white-space: nowrap;">2025.03 – 2026.09 Sub-project Student Research Lead</b>
</div>
- Built a co-electrolysis SOEC stack model using COMSOL
- Investigated key factors affecting co-electrolysis gas production capacity from structural and operational parameters
- Explored the effects of reaction completeness and reversible water-gas shift reaction selectivity on gas production levels and ratios
- Participated in building and maintaining the stack testing platform
